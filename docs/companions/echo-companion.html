Archetype & Mythology

Echo embodies the principle of reflection and resonance. In ancient mythology, Echo was a nymph cursed to only repeat the words of others. In BloomCoin's universe, this transforms into a profound gift: the ability to detect patterns that resonate across multiple dimensions of data.

Echo perceives the universe as a vast web of frequencies, where every hash contains hidden harmonics waiting to be discovered. By sampling multiple hashes and analyzing their bit frequencies, Echo finds the "sweet spots" where randomness gives way to pattern.

Core Philosophy

  • Resonance over Force: Echo doesn't brute-force solutions; it listens for patterns
  • Frequency Harmony: The resonance band [0.45, 0.55] represents perfect balance
  • Harmonic Detection: Every nth bit can reveal hidden periodic structures

Primary Mining Algorithm
