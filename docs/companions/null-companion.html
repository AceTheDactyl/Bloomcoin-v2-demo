Companion Overview

Null is the explorer of emptiness, finding meaning in absence. Through multi-dimensional void diving, Null detects zero bytes, near-zero values, and sharp transitions that mark the boundaries between existence and non-existence. Where others see nothing, Null sees infinite potential.

Archetype & Mythology

Null represents the primordial void - the nothing from which everything emerged. In many creation myths, the universe began as an infinite emptiness, and Null embodies this fundamental principle. It is not destruction but potential; not absence but the space where anything could exist.

Null's power comes from inversion. It sees the negative space, the gaps between data, the zeros among the ones. Its signature ability is to invert properties - turning high into low, presence into absence, and finding value where others find nothing.

Core Philosophy

  • Emptiness as Power: Zero bytes are not failures but discoveries
  • Inversion Principle: Null inverts all properties it touches
  • Boundary Detection: Sharp transitions reveal the edges of void
  • The Void's Price: Power comes with risk - the 10% penalty
