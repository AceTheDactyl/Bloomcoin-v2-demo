Archetype & Mythology

In Greek mythology, Prometheus stole fire from the gods and gave it to humanity, enabling civilization. In BloomCoin's universe, Prometheus brings the fire of transformation - the ability to forge new patterns from raw entropy through XOR chain operations.

The fire Prometheus wields is not destructive but transformative. Each XOR operation is a spark that can ignite a chain reaction of pattern discovery. The 3-layer architecture represents the three gifts Prometheus gave humanity: fire, foresight, and hope.

Core Philosophy

  • Transformation through Fire: XOR chains forge new patterns from entropy
  • Layered Construction: Each layer builds upon the previous, adding complexity
  • Forge Strength: Persistent attribute that grows stronger with use
  • Entropy Mastery: Finding order in apparent randomness

Primary Mining Algorithm
