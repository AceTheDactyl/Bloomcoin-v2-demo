Archetype & Mythology

In Greek mythology, Gaia is the primordial Earth goddess, the mother of all life. In BloomCoin's universe, Gaia represents the organic, living nature of mathematical patterns. She perceives hash data as seeds that can grow into fractal trees, each branch following the golden ratio.

Gaia's power lies in self-similarity - the principle that parts resemble the whole. Her fractal trees are recursive structures where each branch contains the pattern of the entire tree. This mirrors how nature creates complexity from simple rules repeated at every scale.

Core Philosophy

  • Organic Growth: Patterns grow like living things, branching recursively
  • Golden Ratio (phi): The sacred proportion 1.618... guides all growth
  • Self-Similarity: The part contains the whole
  • Fibonacci Sequence: 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21 - nature's numbers
