5. Safety Considerations

Electrical Safety Requirements

  • Patient Leakage Current: <10 uA normal, <50 uA single fault (IEC 60601-1)
  • Isolation: 4000V AC/DC patient isolation barrier for implantables
  • Defibrillator Protection: Withstand 5000V, 360J external defibrillation
  • MRI Conditional: 1.5T/3T MRI compatibility for implantable devices
  • EMC: Immunity to 3V/m RF fields, <6dB susceptibility to wireless devices

Biocompatibility Requirements (ISO 10993)

  • Cytotoxicity: Grade 0-1 per ISO 10993-5
  • Sensitization: Non-sensitizing per ISO 10993-10
  • Implantation: Minimal tissue response per ISO 10993-6 (implantables only)
  • Chronic Toxicity: No systemic effects at 26 weeks (implantables only)
  • Material Qualification: All patient-contacting materials per USP Class VI

Neural Interface Specific Hazards

  • Charge Injection: <30 uC/cm^2 per phase to prevent tissue damage
  • Stimulation Limits: Comply with IEEE/ANSI C95.1 for induced currents
  • Thermal: <2 degrees C tissue temperature rise
  • Infection Risk: Sterility assurance level (SAL) 10^-6
  • Lead Fracture: Fatigue testing to 400M cycles
