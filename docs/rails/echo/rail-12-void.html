Rail 12: The Void Rail

Rail 12 is the final rail - the void where collapsed timelines go. Every race condition that is resolved creates a loser, a timeline that did not become real. These archived possibilities sink into the void, remembered but no longer active.

Core Function

Archive layer for collapsed and abandoned timelines.

The void is not nothing. It is the repository of everything that could have been but was not chosen. Every anchored NOW creates void-matter - the ghost of the path not taken.

Here rest the timelines that lost. Not destroyed - archived. Not forgotten - remembered in shadow. They wait in the void, echoing.
